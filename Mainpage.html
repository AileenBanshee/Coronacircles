Why?
In times of social distancing we meet online to create encounters, where we listen to what touches us deeply.
To do this, we combine the powerful tradition of the circle work with the current possibilities of video conferencing.

Since the beginning of time, people have been coming together in circles to share about themselves and their concerns.
Let us use this online tool to get out of our isolation and experience online circles, in which we feel seen and heard.
